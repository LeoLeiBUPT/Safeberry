Park Renovation Evaluation

Greenfield Community Park, originally established in 1984, has long served as a central gathering place for residents of the Northwood neighborhood. Over the past decade, the park experienced significant wear due to increased foot traffic, aging play structures, and outdated accessibility features. In 2024, the city approved a comprehensive renovation plan aimed at modernizing the park while preserving its natural character.

The renovation project included resurfacing walking paths, installing new inclusive playground equipment, expanding native plant landscaping, upgrading lighting for safety, and adding multi‑use community spaces. This evaluation form is designed to gather feedback from visitors and community members to help assess the effectiveness of these improvements and identify opportunities for future enhancements.
